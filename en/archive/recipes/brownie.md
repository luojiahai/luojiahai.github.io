# brownie

## ingredients

| ingredient       | amount   |
| ---------------- | -------- |
| butter           | 230g     |
| granulated sugar | 230g     |
| cocoa powder     | 50g      |
| eggs             | 3        |
| vanilla extract  | 10ml     |
| salt             | a pinch  |
| flour            | 120g     |
| chocolate chips  | to taste |

## instructions

1. preheat oven to 180°c (356°f).
2. heat and melt the butter until it's very hot.
3. pour butter, granulated sugar, and cocoa powder into a bowl, mix vigorously until the sugar is completely dissolved.
4. add eggs, vanilla extract, and salt, continue mixing until smooth.
5. add flour and chocolate chips, stir until the flour is fully incorporated.
6. pour the batter into a baking pan.
7. bake in the oven for 25 minutes.
