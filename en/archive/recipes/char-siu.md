# char siu

## ingredients

| ingredient         | amount |
| ------------------ | ------ |
| pork shoulder      | 200g   |
| light soy sauce    | 20g    |
| granulated sugar   | 40g    |
| cooking wine       | 10g    |
| oyster sauce       | 10g    |
| red fermented tofu | 1 cube |

## instructions

1. mix light soy sauce, granulated sugar, cooking wine, oyster sauce, and red fermented tofu in a bowl to make char siu sauce.
2. slice the pork shoulder, pour the char siu sauce into a sealed container to submerge the meat, and marinate in the refrigerator for 5 hours.
3. place the marinated pork shoulder in a preheated oven at 200Â°c, take it out every 10 minutes to brush with char siu sauce again, repeat 2 times, for a total of 30 minutes.
