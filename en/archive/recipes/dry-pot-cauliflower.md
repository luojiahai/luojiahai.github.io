# dry pot cauliflower

## ingredients

| ingredient            | amount   |
| --------------------- | -------- |
| cauliflower           | 1 head   |
| pork belly (optional) | 100g     |
| ginger                | to taste |
| garlic                | to taste |
| cooking wine          | 5g       |
| sichuan peppercorns   | 5g       |
| dried chili peppers   | to taste |
| light soy sauce       | 10g      |
| salt                  | to taste |
| black pepper powder   | to taste |

## instructions

1. wash the cauliflower and cut into small florets.
2. slice the pork belly thinly.
3. slice the ginger and mince the garlic.
4. heat a wok with warm oil, add ginger slices and minced garlic and stir-fry. add the pork belly slices and stir-fry, then add cooking wine and sichuan peppercorns until fragrant.
5. once the oil has been rendered from the pork belly, add the cauliflower and stir-fry.
6. add dried chili peppers, light soy sauce, salt, and black pepper powder. continue stir-frying until the moisture from the cauliflower has evaporated.
