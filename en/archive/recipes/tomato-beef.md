# tomato beef

## ingredients

| ingredient      | amount   |
| --------------- | -------- |
| beef            | 500 g    |
| tomato          | 3        |
| onion           | 1        |
| ginger          | 3 slices |
| garlic          | 3 cloves |
| light soy sauce | 10 g     |
| oyster sauce    | 10 g     |
| cooking wine    | 10 g     |
| white pepper    | 5 g      |
| tomato sauce    | 15 g     |
| sugar           | 5 g      |
| cooking oil     | to taste |

## instructions

1. slice the beef thinly and marinate with light soy sauce, oyster sauce, and cooking wine for 30 minutes.
2. wash and cut the tomatoes into chunks, cut the onion into chunks, slice the ginger, and slice the garlic.
3. heat oil in a wok, add ginger and garlic slices and stir-fry until fragrant.
4. add the beef slices and stir-fry over high heat until they change color.
5. add the tomato chunks and stir-fry until the tomatoes release their juice.
6. add the onion chunks and continue stir-frying.
7. season with tomato sauce, oyster sauce, and sugar.
